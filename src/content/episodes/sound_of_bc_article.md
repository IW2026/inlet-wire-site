# A Few New Additions to The Sound of BC

I’ve updated The Sound of BC playlist again with some great new music from the past week or two, and this latest round adds a really nice mix of artists and sounds from across the BC music scene.

The playlist is built around Inlet Wire featured artists, along with other BC artists I keep coming back to. Some have been on the podcast. Some are just artists I think more people should be hearing. The goal is pretty simple: keep one playlist that helps people stay connected to what’s happening here.

This latest update includes Hillsboro, The Abramson Singers, The Zummers, Cherry Pick, Tobacco Brown, and Chief State. It’s a strong reminder of how wide the BC scene really is. Different styles, different approaches, different corners of the province, but all worth spending time with.

What I like about updating this playlist is that it keeps Inlet Wire moving a bit outside the podcast too. Not every artist will show up in the same way. Some come through an interview, some through a recommendation, some through just hearing a track at the right time and wanting to keep it in rotation. This playlist gives all of that a place to live.

So if you’ve been following Inlet Wire through the episodes, this is another good way to keep up with the artists around it. And if you’re just landing on it for the first time, this playlist is a pretty good entry point into the kind of music I’ve been backing lately.

Give The Sound of BC a listen. There are some really good new additions in there. Submissions are welcome: submissions@inletwire.com
